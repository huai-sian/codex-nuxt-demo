## Content Comes First

A content site works best when readers and search engines can quickly understand the page topic. Titles, descriptions, dates, and cover images belong in frontmatter so every page can generate reliable metadata.

## Markdown Publishing

Markdown keeps authoring simple while Nuxt Content turns files into structured data that can be queried during rendering.
